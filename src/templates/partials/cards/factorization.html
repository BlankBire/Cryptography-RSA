Method Overview

Trial Division: Exhaustive search up to √n. Best for very small n.

Fermat: Uses difference of squares—effective when p and q are close.

Pollard's Rho: Cycle finding; works well for medium sized composites.

Quadratic Sieve: Smooth number search, suited for larger moduli.

ECM: Exploits elliptic curves when a factor is significantly smaller.
